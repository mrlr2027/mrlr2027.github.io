Research Scope

Topics we plan to bring together

01

Unified Multimodal Embeddings

A single model that encodes text, images, video, audio, documents, and other modalities into a unified representation space.

02

Cross-Modal Retrieval

Retrieval across modalities, from text-to-video and audio-visual search to document and 3D retrieval.

03

Composed Retrieval

Queries that combine modalities and constraints, including image-plus-text retrieval and interactive refinement.

04

Fine-Grained Search

Instance-level and class-level matching that preserves local, temporal, and identity-sensitive detail.

05

Re-ranking and Efficiency

Late interaction, query expansion, scalable indexing, compression, and multi-stage retrieval pipelines.

06

Evaluation and Applications

Benchmarks, failure analysis, responsible deployment, and retrieval for multimodal RAG and real-world systems.
